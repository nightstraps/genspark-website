Hi There,

We're Genspark.

It only takes one spark to ignite the flame in this generation for positive change and influence in the world. GenSpark is a news awareness website geared towards helping youth find clarity in the world they live in.


The content in current news and media may be challenging for youth to personally relate to. It often portrays senseless content that can be very confusing to understand for younger generations. GenSpark articles are written by teens, for teens to help the youth today relate to everyday world events. GenSpark’s purpose is a safe space for youth to creatively express their thoughts and emotions about events that are happening in the world. Our goal is to create a community among youth where they feel accepted in knowing that they are not going through trials in life alone.The more awareness and exposure to real world events, the more accepting we can be of others, to better the world we live in.
